Glossaire

Meme :

A meme is a content element, in the form of an image, video, or text, primarily characterized by its humorous or sarcastic nature.

Spundtrack :

The soundtrack, or sound track, is the recorded sound portion of a film, or more broadly, an audiovisual production, a live performance, or an artistic exhibition.

Box-office :

The box office is the measure of success for a show, artist, or film calculated based on the generated revenues.

Heterogeneous :

A heterogeneous team is said to be made up of a bizarre blend of different elements.
